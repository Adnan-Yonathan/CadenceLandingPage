FAQ

Good questions.

How does Cadence adapt my plan?add

Each morning you check in — or Cadence reads Apple Health — to gauge sleep, recovery, and body feel. It combines that readiness score with the weather to reshape today's session, then explains the change in plain language.

Do I need a watch or extra gear?add

No. Your iPhone is enough to track runs and check in. If you use Apple Health or a connected watch, Cadence uses that data to fill in recovery automatically.

Is it for beginners or serious runners?add

Both. Whether you're building your first habit or chasing a 5K PR against an Olympian's ghost, Cadence scales the plan to your level and goal.

How does the free trial work?add

The annual plan starts with a 3-day free trial. You won't be charged until it ends, and you can cancel anytime from the App Store before then.

Can I race friends who don't have Cadence?add

Invite them with a share link and race their ghost once they've logged a run. No invite? Race a built-in pace setter or chase your own personal record.
