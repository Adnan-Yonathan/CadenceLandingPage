How does Cadence keep me accountable?add

You set a run alarm and choose the apps you want held. When it’s time to run, Cadence asks you to prove you laced up, then verifies that you completed a real outdoor run before giving your apps back.

How does Cadence verify my run?add

Cadence uses your iPhone to track a real outdoor run. You don’t need a watch or extra gear — just your phone and your running shoes.

Is it for beginners or serious runners?add

Both. Whether you’re building your first running habit or chasing a new PR, Cadence adjusts the plan, accountability, and coaching to your level and goal.
